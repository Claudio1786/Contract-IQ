# Contract IQ: Customer Development Framework\n## Enterprise Procurement Executive Interview Strategy\n\n---\n\n# Executive Summary\n\n**Objective**: Conduct structured interviews with 10+ target procurement executives to validate market assumptions, refine value propositions, and build customer-driven product roadmap.\n\n**Target Profile**: Chief Procurement Officers, VP Procurement, Director of Strategic Sourcing at Fortune 1000 companies with $1B+ revenue and complex B2B vendor relationships.\n\n**Key Validation Areas**:\n1. **Pain Point Validation**: Confirm contract negotiation challenges and quantify impact\n2. **Solution Fit**: Test multi-LLM AI approach resonance and feature priorities\n3. **Pricing Validation**: Validate pricing tiers and ROI assumptions\n4. **Competitive Landscape**: Understand incumbent solutions and satisfaction levels\n5. **Buying Process**: Map decision-making process and key stakeholders\n\n---\n\n# Target Customer Profile & Segmentation\n\n## Ideal Customer Profile (ICP)\n\n### Company Characteristics\n- **Revenue**: $1B - $50B (Fortune 1000)\n- **Industry**: Technology, Manufacturing, Financial Services, Healthcare\n- **Employee Count**: 2,000 - 50,000\n- **Contract Volume**: 500+ vendor agreements annually\n- **Vendor Spend**: $50M - $500M under management\n- **Complexity**: Multi-location, international operations, regulatory requirements\n\n### Procurement Organization\n- **Team Size**: 10-50 procurement professionals\n- **Centralization**: Centralized or center-led procurement model\n- **Technology**: Using CLM systems (Icertis, Ironclad, SAP Ariba)\n- **Maturity**: Strategic procurement vs tactical purchasing\n\n## Target Interview Personas\n\n### Primary: Chief Procurement Officer (CPO)\n- **Decision Authority**: Ultimate budget and technology decisions\n- **Focus**: Strategic cost savings, risk management, digital transformation\n- **Pain Points**: Board reporting, supplier risk, negotiation outcomes\n- **Success Metrics**: Cost savings, cycle time, compliance\n\n### Secondary: VP/Director of Procurement\n- **Role**: Day-to-day operations and vendor relationships\n- **Focus**: Process efficiency, team productivity, vendor performance\n- **Pain Points**: Manual processes, contract review bottlenecks\n- **Success Metrics**: Team utilization, contract turnaround time\n\n### Tertiary: Senior Manager/Director of Strategic Sourcing\n- **Role**: Complex negotiation execution and vendor strategy\n- **Focus**: Contract terms, risk assessment, supplier relationship management\n- **Pain Points**: Contract analysis time, benchmarking data, negotiation preparation\n- **Success Metrics**: Terms improvement, negotiation success rate\n\n---\n\n# Customer Development Interview Structure\n\n## Pre-Interview Preparation\n\n### Research Checklist (Per Interviewee)\n- [ ] Company background: Revenue, industry, recent news\n- [ ] Procurement organization structure and maturity\n- [ ] Current technology stack (CLM, ERP, procurement tools)\n- [ ] Recent supplier/contract management challenges (news/reports)\n- [ ] LinkedIn profile analysis for background and interests\n- [ ] Mutual connections for warm introductions\n\n### Interview Logistics\n- **Duration**: 45 minutes\n- **Format**: Video call (Zoom/Teams)\n- **Recording**: With permission, for analysis\n- **Follow-up**: Summary and next steps within 24 hours\n\n## Interview Guide\n\n### Opening (5 minutes)\n**Rapport Building & Context**\n- Thank you for your time, I appreciate your expertise\n- Quick background: We're building AI-powered contract intelligence for procurement teams\n- I'd love to learn about your current processes and challenges\n- Everything confidential, looking for honest feedback\n- Recording okay for my notes? (Can turn off if preferred)\n\n### Section 1: Current State Assessment (10 minutes)\n**Goal**: Understand current contract management processes and pain points\n\n**Questions**:\n1. **Process Mapping**: \"Walk me through how your team currently handles vendor contract negotiations from start to finish.\"\n   - Follow-up: Who's involved? What tools do you use? Where do delays happen?\n\n2. **Volume & Complexity**: \"How many vendor contracts does your organization negotiate annually? What types are most challenging?\"\n   - Follow-up: What makes certain contracts more complex? Time spent per contract?\n\n3. **Current Tools**: \"What technology does your team use for contract management today?\"\n   - Follow-up: How satisfied are you? What works well? What's missing?\n\n4. **Biggest Challenges**: \"What are the top 2-3 pain points in your contract negotiation process today?\"\n   - Follow-up: Can you quantify the impact? Time, cost, risk?\n\n### Section 2: Problem Validation (10 minutes)\n**Goal**: Validate specific pain points our solution addresses\n\n**Validation Areas**:\n1. **Analysis Time**: \"How long does it typically take to analyze and prepare for a complex vendor negotiation?\"\n   - Probe: Who does this work? What's the process? Bottlenecks?\n\n2. **Benchmarking**: \"How do you know if you're getting competitive terms in your negotiations?\"\n   - Probe: What data sources? Internal benchmarks? Market intelligence?\n\n3. **Risk Assessment**: \"How do you identify and mitigate risks in vendor contracts?\"\n   - Probe: What types of risks? How do you track? Audit process?\n\n4. **Team Efficiency**: \"What percentage of your team's time is spent on contract analysis vs strategic work?\"\n   - Probe: Ideal split? What prevents more strategic focus?\n\n### Section 3: Solution Concept Testing (10 minutes)\n**Goal**: Test our solution approach and feature priorities\n\n**Concept Introduction**:\n\"We're building an AI-powered platform that analyzes vendor contracts and provides intelligent negotiation recommendations. It uses multiple AI models to extract clauses, assess risks, benchmark terms against market standards, and suggest negotiation strategies.\"\n\n**Validation Questions**:\n1. **Initial Reaction**: \"What's your immediate reaction to this concept?\"\n   - Probe: Excitement level? Skepticism? Questions?\n\n2. **Feature Priorities**: \"Which of these capabilities would be most valuable?\"\n   - Contract clause extraction and classification\n   - Risk scoring and compliance checking\n   - Market benchmarking and term comparison\n   - Negotiation strategy recommendations\n   - Cost savings opportunity identification\n\n3. **Multi-LLM Approach**: \"We use multiple AI models - some better at legal analysis, others at financial calculations. Thoughts on this approach?\"\n   - Probe: Value of accuracy? Redundancy concerns?\n\n4. **Integration Requirements**: \"How would this need to work with your existing systems?\"\n   - Probe: CLM integration? ERP systems? Workflow requirements?\n\n### Section 4: Commercial Validation (10 minutes)\n**Goal**: Test pricing, buying process, and value perception\n\n**Questions**:\n1. **Value Quantification**: \"If this solution saved 50% of contract analysis time and improved terms by 5-10%, what would that be worth annually?\"\n   - Probe: Current costs? Savings calculation? ROI threshold?\n\n2. **Budget Reality**: \"What's a reasonable annual investment for this type of solution?\"\n   - Present tiers: $50K (Enterprise), $200K+ (Enterprise Plus)\n   - Reaction? Too high/low? Budget availability?\n\n3. **Buying Process**: \"How would you evaluate and purchase this type of solution?\"\n   - Probe: Decision makers? Evaluation criteria? Timeline? Budget cycle?\n\n4. **Competition**: \"Are you aware of similar solutions? How would you compare?\"\n   - Probe: Current vendors? Satisfaction? Switching considerations?\n\n### Section 5: Closing & Next Steps (5 minutes)\n**Goal**: Secure ongoing relationship and referrals\n\n1. **Interest Level**: \"Based on our conversation, what's your level of interest in seeing this solution?\"\n   - Scale: 1-10, with follow-up on reasoning\n\n2. **Pilot Opportunity**: \"Would you be interested in a 30-day pilot program once we're ready?\"\n   - Conditions? Requirements? Timeline?\n\n3. **Network**: \"Who else in your network might benefit from this conversation?\"\n   - Peer referrals? Industry contacts? Team members?\n\n4. **Follow-up**: \"What would be most helpful as next steps?\"\n   - Demo? Pilot? Industry analysis? Competitive comparison?\n\n---\n\n# Target Interview List\n\n## Tier 1 Targets (High Priority)\n\n### Technology Sector\n1. **Fortune 500 Software Company**\n   - Role: CPO\n   - Company Size: $10B+ Interviewee name, title, background\n- Interview date, duration, format\n- Recording/notes availability\n\n### Current State Analysis\n- Contract volume: Annual vendor agreements\n- Team size: Procurement organization\n- Technology stack: Current CLM/procurement tools\n- Process maturity: Strategic vs tactical procurement\n- Spend under management: Annual vendor spend\n\n### Pain Point Validation\n- Top challenges: Ranked by impact\n- Quantified impact: Time, cost, risk metrics\n- Current workarounds: How they cope today\n- Urgency: Timeline for solution need\n\n### Solution Fit Assessment\n- Feature priorities: Most valuable capabilities\n- Integration requirements: Technical needs\n- Deployment preferences: Cloud vs on-premise\n- Success criteria: How they'd measure value\n\n### Commercial Validation\n- Value quantification: Estimated ROI/savings\n- Budget allocation: Annual investment capacity\n- Pricing reaction: Tier fit and price sensitivity\n- Buying process: Decision makers and timeline\n\n### Competitive Landscape\n- Current vendors: Satisfaction and gaps\n- Awareness: Known competitors and perception\n- Switching barriers: Technical and contractual\n- Evaluation criteria: How they compare solutions\n\n## Analysis Synthesis\n\n### Pattern Recognition\n- **Common Pain Points**: Themes across interviews\n- **Feature Priorities**: Consistent value drivers\n- **Pricing Validation**: Price point acceptance\n- **Market Segments**: Vertical-specific needs\n\n### Persona Refinement\n- **Decision Makers**: Who really buys?\n- **Influencers**: Who shapes decisions?\n- **Users**: Who uses the solution daily?\n- **Success Metrics**: How do they measure value?\n\n### Solution Optimization\n- **Feature Prioritization**: Build vs buy decisions\n- **Positioning Refinement**: Key message testing\n- **Pricing Adjustment**: Market-driven pricing\n- **Go-to-Market**: Channel and sales approach\n\n---\n\n# Outreach Strategy\n\n## findings with market research\n\n### Conflicting Feedback\n- **Analysis**: Segment by persona, industry, company size\n- **Resolution**: Test different approaches with subsets\n\n---\n\n# Expected
