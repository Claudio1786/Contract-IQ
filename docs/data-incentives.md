## Data Contribution Incentives

### Give-to-Get Credits
- Upload **3 contracts** → unlock **6 months of Pro** features.
- Upload **10 contracts** → receive **benchmark deep-dive report** tailored to your segment.

### Referral Loops
- Invite a peer organization; when they activate, both parties receive **benchmark expansion packs** or **additional seats** for 90 days.
- Highlight shareable success metrics (e.g., “Saved $115k on Salesforce renewal”) to drive social proof.

### Outcome Logging Rewards
- After closing a negotiation, log outcome (win/loss, savings %) → earn **insight tokens** redeemable for white-glove audits or advanced analytics.
- Use outcomes to auto-refresh benchmarks and surface case studies within the product.

### Monthly Intelligence Reports
- Contributors receive an exclusive “Contract Intelligence Pulse” covering top clause shifts, pricing trends, and compliance alerts.

### Guardrails
- Explicit consent in onboarding that anonymized, aggregated data powers benchmarks.
- Allow opt-out tiers (at a higher subscription price) to respect sensitivity requirements.
