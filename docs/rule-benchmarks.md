## Rule Libraries & Benchmark Dimensions

### SaaS / Vendor Agreements
- **Auto-Renewal & Termination**
  - Notice periods, escalation formulas, termination-right thresholds.
- **Pricing & Discounts**
  - Base price vs. uplift, tiered usage thresholds, discount depth, CPI links.
- **Liability & Indemnity**
  - Liability caps (absolute, multiples of fees), carve-outs, indemnification scope.
- **Security & Compliance**
  - SOC 2 clauses, breach notification timelines, data residency, DPIA obligations.
- **Benchmark Dimensions**
  - Company ARR band, employee count, industry, vendor category, geography, contract size.
